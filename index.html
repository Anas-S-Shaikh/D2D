What is D2D exactly?

D2D is an on-demand delivery platform that allows you to send or get delivered whatever you want. Just follow the steps required for each option, and once you’re finished, a courier will accept your order and deliver it in minutes. You can track and follow the delivery in real-time. Don’t forget to rate your experience once you receive your delivery.

Hello, we are D2D!

D2D platform eliminates the hassle of sending and receiving packages, couriers, or things you often forget. We connect users, businesses, and couriers to make that possible. The project was born with the aim of transforming the way users acquire what they need, making their cities more accessible.

At D2D, we want to give everyone easy access to get anything, from anywhere within their city, having a sustainable impact on the economy, society, and environment: we are a tech-first responsible company.
